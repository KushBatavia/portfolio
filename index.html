About Kush

Kush is a high school student who picked up design software and programming at 14 and hasn’t really stopped since. He works where design meets development, where form and function stop being a trade-off and start helping each other. Over the years, he’s freelanced, taken on individual projects, and spent two months interning at an advertising agency in Mumbai, slowly climbing what he calls “the design ladder” (mostly a folder of increasingly less embarrassing work).

Outside of design, he’s deep into robotics, has a genuine fondness for chemistry, and harbours a concerning enthusiasm for planespotting and carspotting. His interests are scattered, but tend to converge when he’s sketching ideas on his iPad. Paper sketches exist too, but due to their legibility, they’re best described as “confidential.”

Largely nocturnal, Kush has worked with individuals, small enterprises, and larger corporates to deliver work he’s genuinely proud of. He’s always looking for the next project, and would be happy to help with yours.
